This is a simple ticker of what PredictIt thinks the probability that each candidate wins is, given that they are nominated to their respective parties. The formula is:

$$ P(candidate\ wins\ presidency)/P(candidate\ wins\ nomination) $$

These percentages are probably too high, especially on the high (>70%) end. More on methodology here.

If you think these percentages are wrong, bet against them!
